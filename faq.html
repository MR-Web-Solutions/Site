How long does it take to build a website?

Most standard business websites take around 4–8 business days, depending on the number of pages, content and feedback needed. We will confirm your timeline before work begins.

Do I need to have a domain name already?

No. We can help you choose, acquire and connect a professional domain name for your business.

Will my website work on phones and tablets?

Yes. Every MR Web Solutions website is designed to look and work beautifully across mobile, tablet and desktop devices.

Can you update my existing website?

Yes. We can refresh an existing site, improve its design and speed, or rebuild it with a more modern, professional approach.

Do you provide hosting and ongoing support?

Our Professional and Premium packages include hosting and ongoing support options. We can also provide a maintenance plan for an existing website.

What do you need from me to get started?

Usually your logo, business details, service information, contact details and any photos you would like to use. We will guide you through the rest.

Can I request extra features later?

Absolutely. Features such as booking forms, galleries, blogs, WhatsApp integration and additional pages can be added as your business grows.

How do payments work?

We will discuss the project scope and pricing with you before starting. Larger projects can be structured in clear stages so you always know what to expect.
